Cheap Drugs

A drug cost comparison and pharmacy location app
Purpose:

CheapDrugs is a drug cost comparison and pharmacy location app. CheapDrugs looks at the minimum, wholesale drug price and compares it to the prices charged at the pharmacies located near the user. The user can save their preferred pharmacies in the app, and compare the prices in their area.
In addition to focusing on the cost of a drug, the app also takes into consideration educating the user. The app will integrate the user's insurance information to help them make more informed decisions with their provider based on the coverage that they have for their medications. Our goal is to reduce the time the patient has to wait for their perscription. This can be done by making sure that they are getting the right drug for their coverage, and that the drug is sent to the correct in-network pharmacy, the first time that it is ordered.
With the rise in health care costs, we want to give the power back to the patient so that they can easily find affordable drug prices and educational resources to help them better understand their prescribed health care.

Motivation

Cheap Drugs serves as a hub for prescription information that is friendly to all users. The tools this site provides empowers the consumer to find pharmacies, affordable pricing, and relevant pharmaceutical news.
